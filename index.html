Senior Project: Form Fit

A squat is a seemingly simple movement, but can cause injury if done incorrectly. All levels of fitness can be improved by supporting squat form. This program has been created to identify an individual’s squat form and provide feedback on imperfections of the squat. The program is designed to check the angle of thigh to calf to ensure full muscle activation. In addition, the program would monitor the heel of the individual to recognize any sign of imbalance. In summary this program will give binary, yes or no, feedback; either, approving or disapproving an individual’s squat form.

    Ski Math Addition Game

This was a great experience seeing what it is like working with a team on a large project and working out the complexities of collaboration. My contribution was designing algorithms that autonomously create math questions and available answers. This algorithm was designed to scale with difficulty and saves memory by replacing a hard-coded dictionary of questions and answers.
